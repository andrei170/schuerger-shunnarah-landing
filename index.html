Questions

What injured people ask us

What should I do after a car accident in Columbus, OH?+
Get medical care first, then document the scene and report the crash. Before you speak with any insurance adjuster, talk to a lawyer. We offer a free case review. Call (877) 927-4968 and we will explain your options with no obligation.
How much does it cost to hire Schuerger Shunnarah?+
The initial case review is free. We handle personal injury cases on a contingency basis, which means you discuss fees with us up front and we are paid out of a recovery rather than out of pocket. Every case is different, and we will walk you through the specifics on your call.
What types of accident cases do you handle?+
We handle car accidents, truck accidents, motorcycle accidents, Uber and rideshare accidents, wrongful death, and brain injury claims across Ohio. If you are not sure your situation qualifies, call (877) 927-4968 for a free review.
How quickly should I call a lawyer after a crash?+
As soon as you are able. Evidence fades, insurers move fast, and Ohio has deadlines that can affect your claim. The sooner we review your case, the more we can do. Call (877) 927-4968 or request a free case review on this page.
